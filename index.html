About Open Human

Open Human is a free software 3D model of the human body. There are many textbooks which contain information about the anatomy of the human body, but they do not allow the visualization of the anatomy in 3D space. The goal of the project is to make information about the anatomy of human body more easily accessible to users by presenting it in an intuitive manner.
