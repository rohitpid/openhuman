Rohit Pidaparthi - Founder

Rohit Pidaparthi, a bioengineer conceived the Open Human project after finding anatomy books inadequate for visualizing the human body. Rohit kicked off the oH project by constructing all the 3D models of the human body and writing the original Canvas 3D version of the website. Rohit is the designer and maintainer of the oH3D website and 3D application code, and actively works on the 3D visualization's Javascript code.

Balajee.R.C - 3D App Developer

Helping with the switch from the C3D to O3D technology, Balajee, a piping engineer by day and a 3D graphics developer by night, wrote much of the Javascript for the current 3D visualization. He can be found prowling 3D graphics related IRCs in a state of caffeine induced insomnia during wee hours of the night (IST) under the moniker of 'Hannofcart' or can be reached at balajeerc at gmail dot com.
